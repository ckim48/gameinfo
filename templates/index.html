About Handmade Games

Handmade Games is a platform designed for indie developers, hobbyists, and creators to share and discover unique, self-made games. Whether it's a simple browser game or a full-fledged indie project, we welcome all game developers to showcase their talent.

  • Support for Independent Developers

    Connect with a community of like-minded creators and gamers to get feedback and recognition.

  • Showcase Your Unique Creations

    Share your passion for game development with others who appreciate handcrafted, independent games.

  • Reach New Audiences

    Expand your reach by sharing games designed to help individuals with attention issues, like ADHD, and connect with a community seeking tools to improve focus.
